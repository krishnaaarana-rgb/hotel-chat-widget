System Queries

Can I train it on my own PDFs and URLs?
Yes. Logix ingests your Knowledge Base (Notion, Google Drive, PDFs, Website) and vectorizes it. Updates happen automatically every 6 hours or can be triggered manually via API.
Is my data shared with other models?
Absolutely not. We use isolated containers for every enterprise client. Your data is used exclusively to answer your users and is never used to train our base models.
What happens if the AI doesn't know the answer?
We implement "Graceful Handoff." If the confidence score drops below a set threshold (e.g., 85%), the agent automatically collects the user's details and routes the transcript to a human support queue via Zendesk or Slack.
