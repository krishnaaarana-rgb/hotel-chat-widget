The 4 Core Workflows

A detailed breakdown of the automation logic.

Workflow 1: Cold Contact Sourcing

Trigger: Manual/Scheduled
Process
  1. Config Load: Reads Industry, Job Titles, Company Size, Location from Google Sheets.
  2. Scraping: Apify scrapes Apollo.io based on params.
  3. Filtering: Keeps verified emails only. Extracts Phone, LinkedIn, Company Data.
  4. AI Generation: Generates personalized email for each contact.
  5. Quality Check: AI reviews email. Flags low quality for regeneration.
  6. Scoring: Assigns Confidence Score (0-100).
  7. Approval: Routes to Google Sheets Approval Queue.
Output & Routing

On Approval: Loads to CRM with custom fields (lead_source, confidence_score, ai_email_template).

CRM Folder Routing:

  • "Cold Leads - High Confidence" (80-100)
  • "Cold Leads - Medium Confidence" (50-79)
  • "Cold Leads - Low Confidence" (0-49)

Final Step: Triggers Email Platform (ROI/Instantly/GHL).

Workflow 2: CRM Contact Updates

Trigger: Manual (Pre-Mailshot)
1. Export & Re-verify

Exports CRM contacts and re-verifies via Apify/Apollo to check if they are still at the same company.

2. Detection Logic

If job changed → Route to Workflow 3.
If outdated → Flag for review.
If valid → Update last_enriched_date.

3. Update Data

Updates CRM with fresh phone, email, and social profiles. Ensures 0% bounce rate.

Workflow 3: Job Change Monitoring

Trigger: Daily
Process
  1. Monitor: Tracks contacts via Apify for job updates.
  2. Detection: Identifies new hires in target positions at target companies.
  3. Signal Strength: Calculates based on days in role, seniority, and company size.
  4. AI Drafting: Generates email referencing the specific move.
  5. Validation: Quality Checker validates email logic.
Output

Action: Updates CRM record.

Routing: Adds to folder "Job Change Alerts - This Week".

Goal: Timely outreach to decision-makers within days of role change.

Workflow 4: News & Signal Monitoring

Trigger: Daily
Signals Detected
Funding
New Stores/Locations
Expansion Plans
Leadership Changes
Process Flow
  • NewsAPI aggregates from sources configured in Sheets.
  • AI classifies relevance and extracts key people (Hiring Managers, Execs).
  • Apify finds contact details for those specific people.
  • AI Generates email referencing the news event.
  • Routing: Adds to specific folders: "Funding - Hot", "Expansion Signals", etc.

The Intelligence System

Configuration (Google Sheets)

No code needed. You control everything here:

  • Sheet 1: Cold Sourcing: Industries, Job Titles, Size, Locations, Exclusions.
  • Sheet 2: Job Changes: Titles to monitor, Refresh frequency.
  • Sheet 3: News Signals: Sources (RSS/URLs), Signal Types, Keywords.
  • Sheet 4: Master Exclusions: Blacklists (Companies/People).
  • + Google Form: For quick additions without opening the sheet.

Approval Workflow

Human-in-the-loop review system.

The Queue Includes: Contact info, Email Draft, Confidence Score, Signal Source.

Actions: Approve / Reject / Notes.

System processes approvals every hour.

AI Quality Checker

Every email is scored (0-20) on personalization, grammar, tone, and clarity.

Score < 12 → Regenerate (Improved Prompt)
Score >= 12 → Approval Queue

Confidence Scoring (0-100)

  • Email Verified 30 pts
  • Data Completeness 40 pts
  • Target Relevance 20 pts
  • Signal Strength 10 pts
