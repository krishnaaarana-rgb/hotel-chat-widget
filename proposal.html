The 4 Core Workflows

Powered by n8n, Apify, and AI. Configurable via Google Sheets.

THE ENGINE
1

Cold Contact Sourcing

Apify scrapes Apollo.io based on your filters (Industry, Job Title, Size).

  • AI Email Gen: Personalized templates created automatically.
  • Scoring: Leads routed to High/Med/Low confidence folders.
  • Quality Check: AI reviews relevance before saving to CRM.
2

CRM Hygiene & Updates

Triggered manually before mailshots to ensure zero bounces.

  • Re-Verification: Checks if contact is still at the company.
  • Auto-Enrich: Updates phone numbers and social profiles.
  • If job change detected, auto-routes to Workflow 3.
3

Job Change Monitoring

Tracks key decision makers. When they move, we strike.

  • Detection: Monitors specific contacts for role changes.
  • Contextual Email: AI writes "Congrats on the new role at [Company]..."
  • Alerts: Adds to "Job Change Alerts - This Week" folder.
HIGH INTENT
4

News & Signal Monitoring

NewsAPI aggregates funding, expansion, and hiring signals.

  • Signal Types: Funding, New Stores, Leadership Changes.
  • Decision Maker Hunting: Finds the right person to contact based on the news.
  • Categorization: Auto-sorts into "Buying Signals" folders in CRM.
