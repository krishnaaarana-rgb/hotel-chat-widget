Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need technical skills to install this?

No. We handle the setup for you. We provide a single line of code that you copy and paste into your website builder. If you don't know how, we will do it for you for free.

Does it support my language?

Yes. The AI automatically detects the language of the guest and responds in that language, whether it's Spanish, French, German, Chinese, or 90+ others.

What happens if the AI doesn't know the answer?

It is trained to never "guess." If it doesn't know the answer based on your database, it will politely ask the guest for their email so a human manager can follow up.

Can I approve custom discounts?

Absolutely. We set rules during the "Brand Calibration" phase. You can tell the AI, "Offer a 10% discount if they book 3+ nights," and it will execute that policy flawlessly.

Can I see what guests are asking?

Yes. We provide transparent access to conversation logs via a private Airtable link, so you can see exactly what your guests are interested in.

Does it integrate with my calendar?

The AI focuses on Q&A and Lead Capture. When a guest is ready to book, the AI guides them directly to your existing booking engine link (SynXis, Cloudbeds, etc.) to finalize the reservation.
