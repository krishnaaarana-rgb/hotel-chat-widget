Do I need technical skills to install this?

No. We handle the setup for you. We provide a single line of code that you copy and paste into your website builder. If you don't know how, we will do it for you for free.

Does it support my language?

Yes. The AI automatically detects the language of the guest and responds in that language, whether it's Spanish, French, German, Chinese, or 90+ others.

What happens if the AI doesn't know the answer?

It is trained to never "guess." If it doesn't know the answer based on your database, it will politely ask the guest for their email so a human manager can follow up.
